Nova, Makes your Kanban process easy!

Nova is a Kanban-application developed by 4 students attending Kristiana College in Oslo, Norway. We set out to create what in our eyes would be the perfect Kanban-application with a easy to use and clean design. Nova draws its inspiration from the astronomical definition:

Nova is an exploding star whose luminosity temporarily increases from several thousand to as much as 100,000 times its normal level.

Much in the same way as the stars increase their luminosity, we hope that you can use this application to make your projects explode with brand new, genius ideas, that may end up shining so bright that everyone can experience them.
